# KwachaLite Features

KwachaLite is a comprehensive finance management platform designed for personal and business use. Here are some of the key features:

## Core Features

- **Multi-Workspace Management**: Seamlessly switch between your personal and business finances.
- **Transaction Tracking**: Log your income and expenses effortlessly. AI-powered suggestions help you categorize expenses from receipts.
- **Budgeting**: Create and manage weekly or monthly budgets for different spending categories to stay on top of your finances.
- **Financial Goals**: Set and track your savings goals. You can even create itemized shopping lists for your goals.
- **Bill Management**: Keep track of all your upcoming and recurring bills in one place.
- **Loan Management**: Monitor your loans and repayment schedules.
- **Business Tools**: Manage your clients, and products, and create and send professional quotations.
- **Financial Analytics**: Visualize your financial data with insightful charts and graphs.

## Upcoming and Future Features

We are constantly working on new features to make KwachaLite even more powerful. Here's a glimpse of what's coming soon:

### Payment Integrations

- **Mobile Money**: We are working on integrating with popular mobile money services like M-Pesa, Orange Money, and MTN Mobile Money.
- **Bank Integrations**: Connect your bank accounts for automatic transaction imports and real-time balance updates.
- **Cryptocurrency**: We plan to support tracking and transactions for popular cryptocurrencies.

### Collaborative Finance

- **Group Savings (Stokvels/Chamas)**: Save together with your friends and family for common goals.
- **Shared Goals**: Collaborate on financial goals with your family and friends, track progress together, and celebrate milestones.

### Advanced AI-Powered Features

- **Predictive Analytics**: Get insights into your future cash flow and spending patterns.
- **Smart Recommendations**: Receive personalized recommendations for budgeting, saving, and investing.
- **Automated Expense Categorization**: Our AI will automatically categorize your expenses from bank transactions and receipts.

### Business and Enterprise Suite

- **Advanced Invoicing**: Create and manage invoices with automated reminders and payment tracking.
- **Inventory Management**: Track your stock levels, manage suppliers, and analyze product profitability.
- **Customer Relationship Management (CRM)**: Manage your customer interactions and sales pipeline.
- **Project Financials**: Track project-specific budgets, expenses, and profitability.
- **Team Expenditure Management**: Set spending limits, manage approval workflows, and monitor team expenses in real-time.
